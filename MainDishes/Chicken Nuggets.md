## INGREDIENTS
  - 1 chicken breast 
  - 1/2 lg. boiled potato (must be fully cooked first) 
  - 1/2 red onion (or 1 tsp onion powder for zero wetness) 
  - 1 egg 
  - 1 tablespoon cornstarch 
  - Pepper to taste



## INSTRUCTIONS
  1. Drop everything in food processor
  2. Blend until paste
  3. Form into nuggets
  4. Cook

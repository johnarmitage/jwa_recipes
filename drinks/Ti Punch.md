# Ti Punch

source: https://punchdrink.com/recipes/jen-akins-ti-punch/

## Ingredients:

  - 1/2 barspoon Sirop J.M
  - 1 small lime coin
  - 2 ounces rhum agricole, preferably Rhum J.M Blanc 100-proof  



1. In a rocks glass, add the lime, then syrup, then the rhum.
2. Swizzle and serve.

# Apple Pie Baked Beans

- 2-28 oz cans Bush's Baked Beans
- 1-14 oz can Apple Pie Filling
- 1 lb breakfast sausage 
- ½ medium onion chopped
- ½ red bell pepper chopped
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 16 oz BBQ sauce (use the cheap stuff)
- 1 Tablespoon yellow mustard
- 1 Tablespoon W Sauce or Worcestershire Sauce
- favorite hot sauce to taste
- liquid smoke if not cooking on smoker

1. Open and drain the cans of baked beans. 
2. Brown the sausage with the onions and bell pepper.
3. Pour the beans into a large dutch oven cast iron pot.  
4. Add the sausage mixture along with the can of apple pie filling. Stir gently to combine.
5. Add the bbq sauce, brown sugar mustard, W sauce, and hot rub and gently stir again to incorporate.
6. smoker at 250 for 2 hours stirring occasionally or heat over medium low on oven

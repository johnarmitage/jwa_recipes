# Pickled Red Onions

originally from here: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3z2jTcsL28g

1/2 cup Apple cider vinegar
1 cup water
1.5 teaspoon salt
Tablespoon sugar
whole peppercorns

1. cut thin
2. boiling water on top.  sit for 1 min
3. strain
4. pack in pint jar
5. add juice
6. let cool completely
7. close jar and put in fridge
